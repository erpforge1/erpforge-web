SAP RAP Interview Questions and Answers

Prepare for SAP RAP interviews with practical questions on business objects, behaviour definitions, projections, validations, determinations and service exposure.

What is RAP?

RAP is SAP’s RESTful ABAP Programming Model for building transactional, service-based applications in modern ABAP environments.

Managed vs unmanaged RAP?

Managed RAP lets the framework handle standard transactional behaviour. Unmanaged RAP gives the developer more control over persistence and legacy integration.

What is a behaviour definition?

It defines transactional capabilities such as create, update, delete, actions, validations, determinations and locking behaviour.

What is a service binding?

It exposes a RAP service definition through a protocol such as OData for consumption by UI or external consumers.
